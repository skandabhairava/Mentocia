The pandemic spared no one from its hardships , especially students . Students writing their Entrance or board examinations had it the worst. From being hounded by constant assignments to worrying about their family members , they had to overcome major amounts o f mental stress in a world where mental health wasn't given due attention . We ourselves were the 10th grade students of the 2020-21 batch and have seen numerous challenges learning online. We felt that the online system of learning however beneficial is also very distracting and degrading to our health. For this we planned a web app that provides useful ideas about the world of mental health along with a few activities the web app prompts the user to do . These are rewarded by the app if the user completes it in the given time. These are then used to ‘Redeem’ fun activities , like eating a chocolate or scrolling through social media for a break .
